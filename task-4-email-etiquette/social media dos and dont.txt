SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS CHECKLIST
For College Students

DO'S
1. Keep your social media profiles clean and professional
2. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
3. Think carefully before posting anything online
4. Report any cyberbullying or suspicious activity immediately
5. Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts

DON'TS
1. Never share your personal information like address or phone number publicly
2. Never post offensive, hateful or inappropriate content
3. Never click on suspicious links sent by unknown people
4. Never share your passwords with anyone
5. Never post anything that could harm your academic or professional reputation
