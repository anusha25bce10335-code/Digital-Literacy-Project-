STAY SAFE ONLINE - Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Never scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers
- Always double check the recipient's UPI ID before sending money
- Never approve a payment request you did not initiate yourself
- Remember - to receive money you never need to enter your PIN
- Use strong and unique passwords for all banking apps
- Enable two factor authentication on all important accounts
- Never click on suspicious links received through SMS or WhatsApp
- Always download apps only from official App Store or Google Play Store
- Never share your Aadhaar number or bank details on unknown websites
- Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious transactions
- Report any cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in
- Call 1930 (24x7 helpline) if you are a victim of online fraud
